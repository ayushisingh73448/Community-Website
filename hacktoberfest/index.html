FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What is Ideathon?

The Ideathon held on December 27-28, 2021 is a live event where "students" (participants) design, develop, and pitch a software or hardware project built. We will provide you with the resources and mentorship needed in order to make this happen!

Do I need to know how to code?

Do not worry! These events are precisely designed to help novice people overcome the barrier of early contributions. There will be people of all levels and for sure you will be able to learn a lot.

Am I eligible to participate?

Students of B.Tech 1st and 2nd year, BBA, BCA, MBA and Diploma are eligible to participate in this event.

How much does it cost to attend?

Absolutely nothing! Ideathon is a completely free event run by a non-profit organization. All resources and support for this entire event will be provided free of charge.

Do I have to sign up somewhere to receive my goodies?

No, you don't have to sign up anywhere .

What language will be spoken at the event?

The event will take place mainly in English.
